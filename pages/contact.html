Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

What services does SPX STUDIO offer?

SPX STUDIO offers a wide range of creative services including website design and development, graphic design, UI/UX design, branding, digital marketing, content creation, and more. We also provide premium templates and resources for businesses and individuals.

How can I purchase premium templates?

You can browse our collection of premium templates on the Premium page. Once you find a template you like, click on "View Details" to see more information and then "Buy Now" to make a purchase. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

What is the license for the templates I purchase?

Our standard license allows you to use the template for a single project or website. If you need to use the template for multiple projects or websites, you'll need to purchase a multiple-use license. All licenses include future updates and technical support for a period of 6 months.

How do I get technical support for my purchase?

Technical support is available for all purchased templates for 6 months from the date of purchase. You can contact our support team via email at support@spxstudio.com or through the support portal on our website. Please include your purchase ID and details of the issue you're experiencing.

Can I request custom modifications to a template?

Yes, we offer custom modifications to our templates for an additional fee. The cost depends on the complexity of the modifications required. Please contact us with details of the changes you need, and we'll provide you with a quote and timeframe for completion.
